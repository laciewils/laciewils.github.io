ABOUT ME.

I am
an Oregonian part designer part coder inspired a remote learner a team player

I love building a beautiful, clean interface that pulls the user in but doesn't affect the usability of the application.

My Journey...

My schooling has been a journey that has led me through a series of subjects. With every class, I learned what I did and didn’t like, and progressed from there. This journey took me through two Bachelor’s Degrees, one concentration, minor, certification, and apprenticeship before finally landing me in a place where I feel “right”.

From Health...

My undergrad experience at UC Berkeley centered around healthcare, but interning experiences showed me that the health care industry didn’t match up with my work environment goals. From there, I took my background in math and science to a completely new field – computer science.

To Computer Science...

This second degree was a change and a different sort of challenge. I earned the degree online so I felt myself being disconnected from peer and teacher support – a departure from my first degree at a traditional university. To maintain a sense of balance, I taught science at elementary schools in the afternoons. I found that the interaction made me feel more fulfilled from day to day. My final project for my computer science degree was a mobile application. I developed the front end and enjoyed it so much that I decided to take a course in Swift mobile development after graduation. Creating websites and applications had always sounded so intriguing to me, but this course left me feeling slightly unfulfilled. I realized that what I wanted was to create the visual aspects of these applications and to work with others, while incorporating my coding skills.

To Design and Development...

I sat down at a café and typed my goals into a search engine. I took down a list of different job titles and programs, and in the next few days had narrowed the list down to one program – Bloc UI Design and Front-End Development. This program had me excited to do my work, and had me finally feeling like my work was “right”. I love building a beautiful, clean interface that pulls the user in but doesn't affect the usability of the application.

To Life Outside of Work.

When I'm not wrapped up in pixels and programming, I love staying active by hiking, running, or going to fitness classes. I'm an avid traveler and live music seeker, and am always up to try a new restaurant. I thrive on exploration.
